Adventures

Exploring is one thing we love doing together as a family. A great way to open up a child’s world to possibility and curiousity. Whether its nature, parks, beaches, biking, science, technology or historical places. Some of these powerful experiences will contribute to personal developments in deep and untold ways. One of many ways to keep our family strong and connected.

The Place To Be

Victoria, Australia

Melbourne, Australia is our beloved home and country. A well known state of its beautiful landscapes, multicultural, world class educational and lifestyle. It is wonderful to think of all the great memories we have created here and will continue to create whilst raising our three young boys.

During every school holidays or special celebrations, we will always find something to do, we tend to explore into new adventures or revisit some of our favourite places.

First State

Sydney, Australia

Road tripping from Melbourne to Sydney for Lenix 10th birthday and explored into some of Sydney's iconic places was an absolutely fantastic experience. You gotta love Scenic World and the amazing blue mountains views.

Unlimited rides on scenic railway down the mountain and the skywalk cable car with a glass bottom going across a very deep gorge with a stunning view of the waterfalls. Then we made a final stop walking along the Sea Cliff Bridge and seeing the beautiful crystal blue water from the top view. Ocean on one side and hills on another. Perfect spot to watch the sunrise over the ocean or explore under the bridge.

Explore The Possibilities

Tasmania, Australia

We took a trip to Tassie for Sonix's 10th birthday and visited some great variety of landscapes and wilderness the island offers. We explored into many watefalls, historical places and parks.

To be honest, it's one of the most unique life experiences we encountered and that was such a pleasant surprise for us. We didn't do too much research before arriving so we're so thrilled to find some helpful people in the area - at one point we thought we were the only survivors. LOL!

The Place Desired By Many

Auckland, New Zealand

New Zealand gave us the reason to order our kids first ever passports to travel to another country. We took a 10 day trip for Jerix's 5th birthday and visited some amazing places, and met some lovely people.

We had a wonderful time together and saw the region's amazing beauty. Our kids especially were so pleasantly surprised with how cool of a city it was. One of our highlights was when I asked our kids how would they describe New Zealand with one word? - "RUGBY" Loved it!

Sunshine State

Queensland, Australia

We were so excited to visit Gold Coast for the first time when we had heard such wonderful things about it especially the beaches and the theme parks. With all the hype surrounding it, we ended up going there twice for Sonix and Lenix's 5th birthdays.

We had an amazing time by visiting different places and trying out new things. Queensland was one of our first interstate trip on the plane together as a family and that definitely made our visit worth it with so much memories.
